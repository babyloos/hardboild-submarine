HOW TO PLAY

⚓ SUBMARINE (AXIS)

  • Drag Engine gauge to set power
  • Hold PORT/STBD to turn
  • Set target depth (drag gauge)
  • Coordinate reveals at depth <10m + speed <5kt
  • Fire torpedoes at convoy ships

🚢 DESTROYER (ALLIES)

  • Protect convoys to the destination
  • Read sonar for submarine positions
  • Drop depth charges above submarines
  • Mission succeeds when majority of convoy arrives
